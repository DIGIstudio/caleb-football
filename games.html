Football

PROGRAMME

ONE TOUCH - players 3-5

One goalkeeper, two players. Goalkeeper starts with the ball and rolls it out to the two players. Two players are only allowed two touches, one to score. When someone scores they go in goal. First to 10. Goals equal one point. Assists one point. Saves one point. Expand and build on these rules for your own liking.

STRIKER V KEEPER - players 2

One goalkeeper, one player. Player starts at a designated distance and does a one two with the keeper as soon as he receives the ball he tries to score. If the ball is kicked out or keeper claims it no goal. Rotate every round. First to 10. Expand and build on these rules for your own liking.

HEADERS AND VOLLEYS - players 3 +

One goalkeeper, two or more players. Goalkeeper plays ball to players. Goals can only be scored from volleys or headers. To score you have to receive a ball in the air and may have as many touches juggling the ball as long as it doesnâ€™t touch the ground before you shoot. Goal scorer switches with keeper. Goals one point. Saves one point. Expand and build on these rules for your own liking.
